Privacy Policy

Beijing Zhuohai Wenyi Technology Co., Ltd. respects your privacy. This policy applies globally for app and game users.

1. Data Collection

We collect necessary data to provide services. This includes usage analytics, device identifiers, crash reports, and optionally personal info for customer support and billing.

2. Developer Policy & Platform Adaptation

Our apps and games comply with Google Play and Apple App Store developer policies.

  • Google Play: privacy declarations, data safety form, content rating, and advertising ID usage.
  • App Store: App Privacy details, data minimization, and SIAs (Sensitive Info Use).
  • Third-party networks: AdMob, Unity Ads, Facebook Audience Network, etc.

3. Global Privacy Support

We include policy adaptation for GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, PDPA, and other regional requirements. Users can request access, deletion, or correction through support@bjzhuohai.com.

4. Age Management

Age gate and parental consent mechanisms are deployed where required (COPPA for under 13 US, GDPR-K for minors in EEA). Age classification follows each platform's policy.

5. Security

Data is protected using industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We regularly review security measures and update as needed.
